Boxing Stance

Maintain balance, protect your face, and prepare for offense and defense.

Jab

Your most important punch. Fast, controlled, and always ready to lead.

Cross

The power punch thrown from your rear hand, used to break defenses.

Hook

Thrown in a circular motion to catch your opponent from the side.
